What is FinFest for?

FinFest is a site meant to help you find your favourite summer music event in Finland. Different events offer different vibes and art, but here you'll find your favourite.

Flow Festival

Enjoy the urban area of Suvilahti for the last time in Helsinki!

Festivaali Festivaali

Head to Tampere for a set of the your favourite local artists.

ÖFest

Mysterious island festival is calling. Where is the Ö this year?

Ruisrock

See the mix of local and international artists at this July classic in Turku.

When the winter's cold disappears and the rays of sun start to touch the ground, the music scene awakens in the North. From pop to folk to rap, everyone can find the festival that their heart calls to the most. On top of the sweet sounds, people can also ponder on what their favourite setting is. Would you prefer the calm of the open sea, or do you find yourself the happiest in the forest? In the North, you can find out.
